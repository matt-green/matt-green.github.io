Factory Rescue Project

In this project, a group of characters must progressively rescue members of their party as they climb the levels in a factory. They will encounter interchangeable weapons, keys and locked doors, mazes, and powerups that will help them defeat the onslaught of robots in their path.

 

This game is a little diferent than the other two. It is still in development, but unlike my other projects, this will be almost completely self-made, meaning players, enemies, and items will be modeled, animated, and scripted from the ground up. This is my new 'baby' as far as projects go, and I am looking forward to seeing it progress! Check back soon!
